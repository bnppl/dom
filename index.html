Shadow Club Runner

Stickman Rooftop Parkour

Choose Your Skin

Chain rooftop runs, wall-jumps, and rolls. Use arrow keys in game: left/right move, up jump, down roll.

Tip: hit the glowing portal for a Moto-style trial. Lean with left/right and land flat to avoid crashes.

Upgrade Shop

Mystery Keys: 0

How To Play

Goal: keep moving, chain rooftop jumps, then reach the extraction point at the far right side of the map.

Controls

  • P1: Arrow keys (left, right, jump, roll)
  • P2: WASD (A, D, W, S)
  • P3: IJKL (J, L, I, K)
  • P4: TFGH (F, H, T, G)
  • Moto trial: Up accelerate, Down brake, Left/Right tilt to control flips
  • E: Interact with a locked door
  • Esc: Pause or resume the run

Mission Rules

  • Pick up clue markers to reveal missing code digits.
  • Clues stay pinned at the top until their door is unlocked.
  • Walk back over a clue marker to see that clue again.
  • Stand by the brown door area and press E to enter a code.
  • Correct code unlocks the door and gives bonus keys.
  • Ride through the glowing portal to teleport into the Moto trial.
  • Complete the Moto trial finish line to auto-complete this level.

Traps, Checkpoints, and Winning

  • Roll under low lasers and jump over spikes.
  • Touch checkpoints to move your respawn point forward.
  • Getting hit or falling can cost keys and reset your position.
  • You must solve all doors before extraction unlocks.
  • Finish levels to earn bonus keys and unlock new levels.
